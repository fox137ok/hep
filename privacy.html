Privacy Policy

Human Expenditure Program Fan Experience

Last updated

September 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Human Expenditure Program fan site ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit humanexpenditureprogram.art or interact with the embedded game experience. This site is an independent community resource and is separate from the official itch.io distribution channel for the game.

Information We Collect

We intentionally limit the amount of personal data processed. When you use the site, the following information may be handled:

  • Basic server logs: Our hosting provider may record IP addresses, browser types, referring URLs, and timestamps to keep the site secure. Logs are automatically rotated and are not linked to individual identities.
  • Embedded game content: The playable build is served through an itch.io iframe. Itch.io may collect usage or analytics data under their own policies. Review their privacy notice here: itch.io Privacy Policy. We do not receive personal data from itch.io.

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and secure the fan site experience.
  • Monitor aggregate traffic patterns to improve stability and performance.
  • Respond to questions, bug reports, or support requests you voluntarily submit.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. The site does not run advertisements, analytics pixels, or marketing trackers.

Cookies and Third-Party Technologies

We do not set first-party cookies or tracking pixels. Third-party services that power the embedded game or deliver assets (for example, itch.io or jsDelivr for Tailwind CSS) may place cookies or log requests according to their policies. You can manage cookies through your browser or the controls offered by those services.

Data Retention

Server log data retained by our hosting provider is automatically deleted within 30 days unless we need to investigate security events. We do not maintain separate databases containing visitor information.

Children's Privacy

The site and the Human Expenditure Program narrative explore mature psychological themes and are intended for players aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please notify us so we can remove it.

International Visitors

Your information may be processed on servers located in the country where our hosting provider operates. By using the site, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions that may have data protection rules different from those in your home country.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in site functionality, legal requirements, or third-party integrations. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page. Your continued use of the site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
